**Version:** 1.0

**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026

# Overview

VIK is the native utility token of the Vikasa Network ecosystem.

The token powers participation, community rewards, platform utility, ecosystem services, and future governance within the Vikasa Network ecosystem.

The maximum supply is permanently fixed at **24,000,000 VIK**.

No additional VIK tokens can ever be minted after deployment.

# Supply Policy

The VIK token follows a permanently fixed supply model.

Current characteristics include:

- Fixed maximum supply
- No public mint function
- No owner mint function
- Publicly verified smart contract
- No proxy contract
- No transfer tax
- No blacklist functionality
- No trading pause functionality

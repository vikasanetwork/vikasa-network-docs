# Vikasa Network (VIK) Tokenomics

**Last Updated:** 30 June 2026

---

# Overview

VIK is the native utility token of the Vikasa Network ecosystem.

The token is designed to support ecosystem participation, community rewards, platform growth, and future utility across Vikasa Network.

The maximum supply is permanently fixed at **24,000,000 VIK**.

No additional VIK tokens can be created after deployment.

* Fixed maximum supply
* No public mint function
* No transfer tax
* No blacklist functionality
* No trading pause functionality
